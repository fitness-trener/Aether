# Design-Partner Outreach Email Templates

Three templates, calibrated to who's reading. All three end with a
specific 30-minute ask and a calendar link — no "would love to
chat" verbiage.

The implicit attachment in every email is `ONE_PAGER.md` plus a link
to a 60-second demo video (the YC application video works fine).

---

## Template B — security / compliance team at an AI-using enterprise

> Subject: Compile-time attestation for AI-generated production code
>
> Hi `[FIRST_NAME]`,
>
> Quick context: I'm `[FOUNDER]`, building Aether — a programming
> language whose compiler refuses to compile any composition that
> violates declared architectural constraints. The use case I want
> to discuss with `[COMPANY]` is compile-time attestation for code
> your AI agents are shipping.
>
> What you'd get from a 3-month design-partner engagement:
> - A language where every module declares its capability scope
>   (`requires capability log, net.fetch`); the compiler refuses to
>   compose anything outside that scope.
> - Structured diagnostics for every architectural violation, with
>   machine-readable `extra` dicts (CISO-readable audit trail).
> - A 10-task benchmark you can use as a regression suite for your
>   own AI-generated code (one of our 10 tasks is a payment
>   workflow with refinement-typed `Amount`).
>
> What I'd ask in exchange:
> - 30 minutes / week of CISO or staff-engineer time for feedback.
> - Permission to publish a case study post-engagement.
>
> 30 minutes next week to walk through the threat-model framing?
> `[CALENDAR LINK]`
>
> Thanks,
> `[FOUNDER]`
